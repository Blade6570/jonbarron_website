I am a Ph.D student in the Faculty of Computing and Electrical Engineering, Tampere University of Technology (TUT). I am working on computer vision under the supervision of Professor Esa Rahtu from TUT and Professor Juho Kannala from Aalto University.

Prior to joining TUT, I was working as a Researcher at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) Innovation and Research where I was mostly working on sensor signal processing. I obtained my Master's degree from IIT Bhubaneswar in Electronics and Communication Engineering. I did my thesis with Professor Ganapati Panda on compressive sensing.
